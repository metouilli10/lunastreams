Privacy Policy

We value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains what data Luna Streams collects, why we collect it, and how we keep Australian subscribers in control of their personal information.

How we handle your data

Luna Streams collects minimal information to provide fast IPTV activation, resolve support requests, and maintain a safe platform. We never sell personal data and only share it when absolutely required.

  • Personal details: Name, email, billing address, and phone are used to set up your account and deliver support.
  • Technical telemetry: Device type, IP address, and connection logs help us diagnose buffering or access issues.
  • Payment data: Managed by Stripe and PayPal under PCI DSS standards; we do not store full card details on our servers.

Your privacy controls

Access & correction
Email lunastreamsau@gmail.com to request a copy of your data or update inaccurate details.
Deletion
Cancel your subscription and request deletion—we’ll erase personal identifiers within 30 days unless legally required to retain them.
Marketing preferences
Opt-out links appear in every email. You can also manage preferences inside your account dashboard.
Complaints
Escalate concerns to the OAIC (Office of the Australian Information Commissioner) if you’re unhappy with our response.

How we use information

We only use personal data for purposes that align with your relationship with Luna Streams.

  • Account provisioning: Activating test lines, managing subscriptions, and sending service updates.
  • Customer support: Diagnosing issues, confirming identity, and ensuring safe account recovery.
  • Compliance: Meeting obligations under Australian anti-piracy and consumer protection laws.

For billing disputes or consumer law queries, refer to our Refund & Cancellation Policy.

Security measures

We protect your data with:

  • TLS 1.3 encryption on dashboards and apps
  • Role-based access for staff with annual training
  • Offsite, encrypted backups retained for 90 days

Security incidents are reported to affected users and regulators within 72 hours.

When we share data

Trusted partners

  • Stripe & PayPal for secure payments
  • Zendesk (or equivalent) for helpdesk ticketing
  • Content delivery partners to prevent fraud
  • Analytics providers (aggregated, anonymised)

We never share

  • Your data with advertisers without consent
  • Personal details to third parties for resale
  • Credentials or playlists outside our secure systems
  • More data than legally required when responding to authorities

Every vendor we work with provides written privacy assurances and aligns with GDPR as well as Australian Privacy Principle 8 (cross-border disclosure).

Detailed privacy statements

Below you’ll find the full legal wording of our Privacy Policy.

1. Data controller

Luna Streams Pty Ltd (ABN available upon request) is the data controller for services delivered to Australian residents.

2. Lawful basis

We process data on the basis of contract fulfilment, legitimate interests (service improvement), and explicit consent for marketing communications.

3. Data retention

Account records are retained for the duration of your subscription plus five years to meet tax and audit requirements. Support communications are stored for 24 months.

4. International transfers

If we transfer data outside Australia (e.g., EU or US data centres), we ensure comparable privacy safeguards, including standard contractual clauses where needed.

5. Cookies & tracking

Cookies personalise your dashboard and remember device preferences. You can disable non-essential cookies via browser settings or our consent banner.

6. Children’s data

Luna Streams is not directed to children under 16. If we discover we’ve collected data from minors without parental consent, we will promptly delete it.

7. Data breaches

We maintain an incident response plan. If a breach occurs, we will notify affected individuals and relevant authorities per OAIC guidelines.

8. Contact details
