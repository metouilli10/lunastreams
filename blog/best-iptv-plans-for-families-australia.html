What Australian families need from IPTV

  • Simultaneous streams for parents, teens, and youngsters.
  • Kid-friendly channels with classification filters.
  • Access to sports, movies, and educational content.
  • Reliable customer support in Australian time zones.
  • Fair pricing in AUD and the ability to pause or downgrade plans.

Managing multi-device streaming

To keep streams smooth:

  • Create profiles or device labels within your IPTV app to track usage.
  • Schedule automatic logouts on lesser-used devices.
  • Optimise your network using the recommendations in our NBN speed guide.

Setting parental controls

Luna Streams supports PIN-protected profiles and content ratings. To keep viewing safe for children:

  1. Enable parental PIN inside your IPTV player settings.
  2. Set age categories to block MA15+ or R-rated content.
  3. Create a dedicated kids’ channel list with curated favourites such as ABC Kids, Nickelodeon, and Disney.

Channel ideas by family member

  • Parents: Lifestyle (HGTV, BBC Lifestyle), documentaries (Nat Geo, History), Aussie news (ABC, SBS).
  • Teens: Sports (ESPN, Fox Footy), music (MTV), sci-fi (SyFy, FX).
  • Kids: ABC Kids, Cartoon Network, Boomerang, Disney Junior with language learning options.
