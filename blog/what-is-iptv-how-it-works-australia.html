What is IPTV?

Internet Protocol Television (IPTV) delivers live TV, on-demand video, and catch-up content through internet connections instead of traditional broadcast signals. In Australia, IPTV services like Luna Streams use secure streaming servers positioned close to the NBN backbone to provide high-quality HD and Full HD channels without the need for satellite dishes or foxtel cables.

Unlike over-the-top apps that focus on single networks, an IPTV subscription Australia bundles dozens of channel groups, sports leagues, and entertainment packages into one platform. You access these channels through a dedicated IPTV app or IPTV box that communicates with the IPTV provider's servers.

How IPTV works for Australian viewers

With Luna Streams, your content is delivered from Australian-optimised servers through encrypted streams. When you open the Luna Streams IPTV app, it authenticates your subscription, fetches the Electronic Program Guide (EPG), and loads your customised channel list. The content itself is transmitted using adaptive bitrates to keep playback smooth on Aussie NBN connections, even when speeds fluctuate at peak times.

  • Our CDN routes the stream through the closest Australian or New Zealand node.
  • The IPTV player on your Smart TV, Firestick, or mobile selects the most stable bitrate.
  • You can pause, rewind, or switch channels instantly without resynchronising hardware.

IPTV vs traditional cable and satellite

Traditional cable and satellite rely on physical infrastructure like Foxtel cables or rooftop dishes. IPTV streams everything over the internet, which means you can watch on any device with an app and a stable connection. For Australian households in apartments or rentals where installing hardware is a hassle, IPTV is a simpler alternative to Foxtel.

  • International channels: Access global sports and entertainment in addition to Australian free-to-air.
  • Integrated VOD: Watch on-demand films and series within the IPTV interface.

Troubleshooting and NBN tips

Because IPTV relies on your internet connection, ensuring your NBN plan is fast enough is essential. We recommend at least 25 Mbps for HD streaming and 50 Mbps for multi-device households. If you experience buffering:

  • Connect via Ethernet instead of Wi-Fi when possible.
  • Restart your modem and router to clear congestion.
  • Switch between main and backup Luna Streams servers from within the app.
