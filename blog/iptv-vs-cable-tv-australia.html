IPTV vs Cable TV - What's the Difference?

IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) delivers TV content over the internet, while Cable TV (like Foxtel) uses physical cables to transmit signals. In Australia, IPTV services like Luna Streams are rapidly replacing traditional cable TV subscriptions due to better value, more flexibility, and superior channel selection.

Key differences between IPTV vs Cable TV:

  • Delivery Method: IPTV uses your internet connection; Cable TV requires physical installation
  • Pricing: IPTV typically costs 50-70% less than cable TV packages
  • Channels: IPTV offers 20,000+ channels vs cable's limited packages
  • Flexibility: IPTV has no contracts; Cable TV often requires 12-24 month commitments
  • Setup: IPTV setup takes minutes; Cable TV requires technician installation
