Traditional cable packages often exceed $100 per month once sports and premium add-ons are included. IPTV plans start under $30 per month, allowing households to reinvest savings into faster internet or streaming accessories. With flexible terms and no installation fees, IPTV reduces long-term costs and eliminates hidden charges.
