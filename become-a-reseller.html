Reseller FAQs

Answers to the most common questions about joining the Luna Streams reseller channel.

What internet quality do my customers need?

Recommend at least a stable 16 Mbps connection for HD streaming. Higher speeds improve clarity and reduce buffering during peak events.

Which payment methods can I use?

You can fund credits with PayPal, major credit cards, or approved crypto wallets. After payment, we email portal credentials (M3U, Xtream codes, EPG URLs) within minutes.

How do I set up customer devices?

Follow our device tutorials, chat with reseller support, or email the onboarding team. We’ll help activate Smart TVs, Firesticks, MAG, Android, VLC, and more.

Which devices are supported?

Luna Streams works on Smart TVs, Fire TV, Android TV boxes, MAG, Enigma, mobile apps, desktop players, and Kodi.

How quickly will customers receive access?

Orders typically process within 3 minutes to 4 hours. During busy periods (evenings or weekends) allow up to 2 hours.

When does the subscription clock start?

Time begins once credentials are delivered. If emails arrive later, the subscription start automatically adjusts so your customers receive the full duration.
